Chicken Ceaser Salad

Ingredients

- 3 slices of bread, torn to small bite size pieces
- olive oil spray
- 4 slices prosciutto, excess trimmed
- 400g worth of chicken breast
- 300g worth of lettuce chopped to bite size chunks
- 50g of fresh parsley leaves finely chopped
- 3 hard boiled eggs peeled and chopped to quarters
- 1 tbsp parmesan cheese, finally grated
- 150ml ceaser dressing

StepbyStep

1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line 2 baking trays with non-stick baking paper. Place bread on 1 tray. Spray lightly with oil. Place prosciutto on the remaining tray. Bake, turning bread once, for 7-8 minutes or until the bread and prosciutto are crisp and golden. Set aside to cool.
2. Meanwhile, spray a non-stick frying pan with oil. Heat over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 4-5 minutes each side or until golden and cooked through. Set aside to cool slightly. Thinly slice.
3. Break the prosciutto into shards. Combine the bread, prosciutto, chicken, lettuce, parsley, egg and parmesan in a serving bowl. Drizzle over the dressing.

Chicken Soup

Ingredients

- 1 rotisserie chicken shredded
- 1 onion finely chopped
- 3 carrots chopped
- 3 celery sticks chopped
- 3 cloves of garlic finely chopped
- 100g of freshly chopped dill
- olive olive extra virgin
- chicken broth

StepbyStep

1. Heat up pot to a high heat. Add 20ml olive oil, oinions, salt. When onion is translucent add garlic, celery and carrots, mix then add broth.
2. Add shredded chicken along with salt and pepper.
3. Stire gently for 10 minutes and add dill.
